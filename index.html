About Me

I was born in Florida but raised in Taiwan. For my childhood, I did both video games and sports alot. Basketball, badminton, swimming etc. I was also in the swimming team for 3 years during junior high. At the age of 15 I moved back to Florida to attend high school. I lived with the host family provided by my school and that was the last time I ever saw my family. Just kidding.. I go back to Taiwan to visit them twice a year. It's my 8th year living in the states now and I've learned to become independent, seen the diversity of the culture and expereienced lots of the extraordinary things this big big world has provided for us. My life is just getting started righ now and I'll always be looking foward to the future with the best of me.
